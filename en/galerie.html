Movie gallery

If the realism and cruelty of Boyz N The Hood has impacted you, and you're looking for more, there are plenty of films that take up its themes in their own way and explore other subjects.

Just Mercy

Destin Daniel Cretton

Attorney Bryan Stevenson fights to defend the wrongfully convicted in Alabama. He'll have to overcome a pronounced racism in a hostile legal system in order to fight for justice.

CHECK IT OUT

Far From Heaven

Todd Haynes

In the 1950s, Cathy Whitaker discovers that her husband is having a homosexual relationship with another man. Isolated and rejected, she eventually befriends a black man despite what society told her.

CHECK IT OUT

White Dog

Samuel Fuller

Right after a car accident, a white dog, who has been trained to attack black people, is taken in by Julie, a young woman determined to change him.

CHECK IT OUT

Do The Right Thing

Spike Lee

On a hot day in Brooklyn, young Mookie works as a delivery boy at a local pizzeria. As everyone goes about their business, the summer heat begins to exacerbate racial tensions.

CHECK IT OUT

The Hate U Give

George Tillman Jr.

After witnessing the death of his childhood friend at the hands of a police officer, Starr tries to find a way to fight for what's right despite pressure from his community.
